Color Picker Game

Objective of the game:

Test your knowledge of RGB values!

Rules of the game:

1. Let me give you a quick brief of RGB value. In RGB value, R stands for Red, G for Green and B for Blue.

2. In this game, the player will get a random RGB value.

3. The player have to guess the color corresponding to that RGB value.

4. If the player guessed correctly then he/she win otherwise they will get another chance to guess.

5. The game consist of two modes. In easy mode the player have to guess among three cards whereas in hard mode player have to guess among six cards.
